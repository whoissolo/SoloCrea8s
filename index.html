Here's all the stuff I do.

I am a multidisciplinary creative professional specializing in photography, cinematography and video editing. Working fluidly across commercial, narrative, documentary, and event projects, brining stories to life through every stage of production, from initial capture to final delivery. Combining technical expertise with creative vision to craft compelling visual content that connects with audiences and serves each project's unique goals.

Photography

Capturing compelling still images that tell stories, evoke emotion, and communicate ideas. From commercial product shoots to editorial portraits, documentary work to event coverage, creating images with strong composition, lighting, and visual impact that serve each projects's unique needs.

Cinematography

Crafting the visual language of moving images through camera work, lighting, and composition. Bringing narratives to life with intentional shot selection, movement, and atmosphere, whether for commercial spots, narrative films, documentaries, or branded content. Translating creative vision into cinematic reality.

Video Editing

Shaping raw footages into cohesive, engaging stories through post-production. Building narrative flow, rhythm, and emotional resonance through cutting, color grading, sound design, and effects. Taking projects from rough assembly to polished final product across all formats and genres..
